Volunteer Opportunities

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a difference in our community while developing valuable skills and connections.

Note: Membership is required to volunteer for these positions.

Event Coordinator

Help plan and organize chapter events, workshops, and tech talks.

Responsibilities:

  • Coordinate with speakers and presenters
  • Manage event logistics and scheduling
  • Promote events through various channels
  • Collect feedback for future improvements
Time Commitment: 3-5 hours per week

Outreach Volunteer

Participate in community outreach programs to promote computing education.

Responsibilities:

  • Visit local schools for tech demonstrations
  • Assist with coding workshops for K-12 students
  • Create educational materials
  • Mentor younger students interested in computing
Time Commitment: 2-4 hours per week

Content Creator

Create content for our website, social media, and newsletter.

Responsibilities:

  • Write blog posts about chapter activities
  • Design graphics for social media
  • Create promotional materials for events
  • Maintain and update the chapter website
Time Commitment: 2-3 hours per week

Mentorship Program Assistant

Help coordinate and support our mentorship program.

Responsibilities:

  • Match mentors with mentees
  • Organize mentorship activities and check-ins
  • Collect feedback from participants
  • Develop resources for mentors and mentees
Time Commitment: 2-4 hours per week

Benefits of Volunteering

Volunteering with ACM-W offers numerous benefits beyond the satisfaction of giving back to the community.

Professional Development

Gain valuable skills and experience that will enhance your resume and prepare you for your future career.

Networking

Connect with faculty, industry professionals, and peers who share your interests and can provide mentorship.

Leadership

Develop leadership skills by taking on responsibilities and working collaboratively with a diverse team.
