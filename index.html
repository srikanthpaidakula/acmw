About SR University ACM-W Chapter

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates for the full engagement of women in all aspects of computing. Our chapter provides opportunities for personal growth, networking, and professional development.

Community

Join a supportive network of peers, mentors, and industry professionals passionate about technology.

Education

Access workshops, tech talks, and resources to enhance your technical and professional skills.

Recognition

Celebrate achievements and contributions of women in computing through awards and scholarships.
